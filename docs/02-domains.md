# Domain Breakdown

## 1. Identity & Access

Handles:
- Registration
- Login
- Role management
- Protected routes

---

## 2. Catalog

Handles:
- Categories
- Meals
- Provider menus
- Filtering

---

## 3. Cart & Checkout

Handles:
- Cart (frontend state)
- Checkout flow
- Order creation

---

## 4. Orders

Handles:
- Order lifecycle
- Status transitions
- Customer tracking
- Provider fulfillment

---

## 5. Reviews

Handles:
- Customer feedback
- Purchase validation
- Rating system

---

## 6. Admin Governance

Handles:
- User management
- Category management
- System monitoring

---

## Key Principle

Each domain is isolated by responsibility and enforced through role-based access control.
